# Prototype character asset

`elf-ranger.glb` is the animated Soldier example distributed with three.js and
originates from Quaternius' **Ultimate Animated Character Pack**, released under
CC0 1.0. It is used here as a replaceable prototype mesh. The elf ears, ranger
equipment, rendering, locomotion controller, environment, and effects are built
in the Sanctum & Shadow prototype code.

Source: https://quaternius.com/packs/ultimatedanimatedcharacter.html
