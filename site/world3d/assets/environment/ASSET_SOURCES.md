# Environment asset sources

All third-party environment models in this directory are used under CC0.

- `medieval-village/`: Quaternius, [Medieval Village Pack](https://quaternius.com/packs/medievalvillage.html), December 2020. Authored house, inn, bell tower, and blacksmith models. The original `License.txt` is stored with the assets.
- `stylized-nature/`: Quaternius, [Ultimate Stylized Nature Pack](https://quaternius.com/packs/ultimatestylizednature.html), May 2022. Authored glTF birch trees and vegetation with texture and normal maps. The original `License.txt` is stored with the assets.
- `cave/`: OrbitStudios / Fernando Ferreira, [LowPoly Cave Entrance](https://opengameart.org/content/lowpoly-cave-entrance), released under CC0 on OpenGameArt.
- `forest-ground/`: Rob Tuytel / Poly Haven, [Forest Ground 01](https://polyhaven.com/a/forrest_ground_01), 1K diffuse, OpenGL normal, and roughness maps, released under CC0.

Only the small runtime subset used by the Vaelthar vertical slice is loaded by the game. The cave archive also retains its original source and preview files for provenance; they are not fetched by the runtime.
